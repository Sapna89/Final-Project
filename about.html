About Us

Transparency is a studio based out of Bangalore. We specialize in creative documentary photography. At heart, we are story tellers through images.

We are dedicated to our art with a camera in hand. We will do anything and everything to frame images. Images that tell your story. The images that reflects you and your style. Memories(Images) you will love forever. We are dedicated to frame images which will be preserved as a memory for generations.

Though we are based out of Bangalore our assignments have taken us to Goa, Most places in Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Orrisa, Rajasthan, Kolkatta, Delhi…………

So, whether your event is at an exotic location or at a backyard of your house, we would love to be a part of it and document it.
